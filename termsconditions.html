TERMS AND CONDITIONS:

  1. CONTRACT:

    2. A contract which follows immediately from the previous contract with no break in service.
    e.g. If one contract ends in 31stoctober than a new contract will begin from 1stnovember
    of the same year.

    • NOTE: Minimum contract period of a teachers should be atleast one year.

  2. PAYMENT:

    • Each student have to pay it's fee online( as per given bankaccount on website).

    • Each teachers will get their payment online itself.For this teachers have to provide
      their bankaccount number.

  3. DOCUMENT:
    • Certificates(upto highest course)
    • Aadhar card
  4. POST OF RESPONSIBILITY:

    5. A person can't be appointed to a post of responsibility unless then are registered.

  5. PAID LEAVE AND ABSENCE:

    6. All teachers on any form of paid leave of absence must be registered with the teaching council
    and maintain the registeration at all time.In the event if a person registration lapses while on
    paid leave,salary will not be paid for the period he/she is unregistered and the person remains on
    the original leave type.

  6. APPOINTMENT OF A REPLACEMENT TEACHER:

    7. Where a replacement teacher is to be employed to cover TCNR leave,he/she shall be affected a
    fixed term/specified puprose contract to be terminable on the return/resignation/retirement/end
    of contract, of the person on leave but no later than the end of the school year.

  7. RESUMPTION OF DUTY:

    8. A person on TCNR leave must notify the employer immediately upon registration with the Teaching
    Council and a date shall be arranged with the employer for the earliest possible return to duties.
